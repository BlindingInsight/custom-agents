Results

Retail

Enterprise demand forecasting for a major US retailer

Problem

Inconsistent forecasts across merchandising, supply chain, and finance. Overstocks in some locations, stockouts in others. A prior initiative had floundered for two years.

Solution

Unified forecasting platform covering 200K SKUs across 2,000+ stores and online channels. Scaled team from 5 to 45.

Result

$3-4B in free cash flow unlocked through inventory optimization and better planning.

Retail

Clearance pricing optimization after prior failed attempts

Problem

Multiple prior attempts at pricing optimization had failed (external vendors, internal teams). Merchants had lost trust.

Solution

Embedded optimization directly into merchant workflows. Merchants see recommendations alongside projected margin impact.

Result

$100M+ profit impact. Rebuilt trust by letting results speak.

Retail Media

Scaling retail media to high-margin revenue

Problem

A major grocer needed to scale its advertising business but lacked the analytics to optimize it.

Solution

Experimentation engines, forecasting models for ad inventory, and optimization to match budgets with customer segments.

Result

30% CAGR at 50% margin.

Manufacturing

Operations platform for a made-to-order factory

Problem

Zero production visibility. Paper-based scheduling. No data infrastructure. Every late piece is scrap.

Solution

Real-time operations platform for production scheduling, machine utilization, scrap analytics, and delivery predictions.

Result

Live on the factory floor. Real-time intelligence where there was none.
