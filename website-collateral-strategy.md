Last updated: 2026-03-19

## Content Format Per Vertical

Each vertical landing page should have:

1. **Hero statement** (1 sentence): what problem we solve in this industry
2. **3-5 case study cards**: each with title, one-paragraph summary, key metric (if available), and a "learn more" link
3. **Capabilities list**: bullet points of what we can do (drawn from Technology Solution fields)
4. **Call to action**: "Let's discuss your [industry] AI challenges"

## Case Study Page Format

Each case study (when clicked from a card) should have:

1. **Title**
2. **The problem** (from Business Case field, written for the industry audience)
3. **What we built** (from Technology Solution field, business language only)
4. **What made it work** (from Innovation Highlight field)
5. **Impact** (if available, quantified)
6. **Technical approach** (from Technology Used/Tools, for technical readers who scroll down)

## Relationship to Pitch Deck

The pitch deck (for in-person meetings) should mirror the website structure:
- One slide per vertical with the lead case study
- A "portfolio" slide showing all 10 themes
- Detailed case study slides in an appendix for deep-dive conversations
